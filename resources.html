Government eCARe Portal

The Indian Ministry of Health utilizes the eCARe online platform to pre-clear remains arriving from international destinations.

  • Required: Death cause certificate
  • Required: Embalming details
  • Required: Coffin sealing details
  • Required: Indian Consulate NOC

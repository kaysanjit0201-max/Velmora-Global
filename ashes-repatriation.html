Process Overview

Urn Sealing Certificates & Airport Clearances

Carrying or shipping cremated remains (ashes) requires specific legal certificates to pass airport security scans, port health checks, and customs border control. While ashes do not carry biological risks like human remains, international laws treat cremated ashes as cargo that must be certified to prevent illegal transport.

Velmora Global International secures all required documents for carrying ashes. We obtain the Cremation Certificate, the Urn Sealing Certificate from the crematorium (confirming the urn contains *only* cremated remains), and the Indian Consulate NOC. We advise families on airline container requirements: the urn must be made of scan-friendly non-metallic materials (such as wood, marble, ceramic, or glass) so it can clear X-ray security scanning.

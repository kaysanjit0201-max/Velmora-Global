Briefing Guide

What Details to Have Ready

Having these documents ready helps our coordinators register the case with consulates immediately.

Deceased Records

Deceased's full passport scans, original citizenship document files, and municipal birth records.

Medical cause Certificate

Official medical cause of death declaration (cause must be certified as non-contagious/non-infectious).

Authorizing relative

Signed next-of-kin letters appointing Velmora Global to handle clearances at embassies.

Embassy Clearances

Aviation packaging standards

Before boarding caskets on flights, airlines require hermetic seals and custom wooden shipping boxes.

Hermetic Zinc Seal

The casket inner liner must be airtight (certified by embalming registries) to comply with flight rules.

Outer Wooden crate

Caskets are secured inside robust outer plywood crates to prevent shipping damage in commercial cargo holds.

Consular Stamps NOC

Embassy passport cancellation stamps and death registries NOCs secured by daily courier dispatches.

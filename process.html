Phases of Coordination

How We Manage Your Case

From initial hospital dispatch to cargo airport handoff, we handle every detail so families can focus on grieving.

1

Case Intake & Registration

We gather the deceased's passport details, death notification logs, and next-of-kin letters. Our New Delhi team initiates local embassy notifications and reserves urgent mortuary holds.

2

Custom Clearance & Translation

We dispatch legal runners to embassies to secure death registration stamps, passport cancellations, and consulate NOC certificates. If required, certified translation of coroner records is processed.

3

Sanitation, Embalming & Casket Sealing

Our certified medical partners perform sanitation and embalming in compliance with WHO standards. Remains are secured in a hermetic zinc liner inside a high-grade wooden casket.

4

eCARe & APHO Clearance Filings

For India entries, documents are uploaded directly to the Government eCARe portal. We secure pre-approvals from Airport Health Officers (APHO) to guarantee instant clearances upon flight landing.

5

Cargo Freight Booking & Handoff

We secure space on key commercial cargo airlines (Emirates, Air India, BA) under climate-controlled conditions. The casket box is delivered to customs terminals for security scanning and boarding.

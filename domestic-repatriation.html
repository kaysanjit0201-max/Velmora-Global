Process Overview

Interstate Road & Airline Cargo Desks

Transporting deceased remains across different states within India involves navigating municipal clearances, local police station NOCs, embalming certifications, and airport security checks. For distances exceeding 500 kilometers, air cargo is the preferred route, utilizing passenger airlines (Air India, Indigo) to minimize delay and ensure dignity.

Velmora Global International manages the complete interstate domestic repatriation. We arrange the coroner's certificate verification, register the embalming certificate, pack the casket according to IATA standards, coordinate air waybills, and handle the pickup and drop-off coordinates at major Indian airports.

Embassy Clearances

Aviation Packaging Standards

Before boarding caskets on flights, airlines require hermetic seals and custom outer wooden boxes.

WHO Health Seals

Embalming certificates issued by registered doctors in compliance with WHO quarantine instructions.

Hermetic Zinc Liner

Caskets must be sealed airtight (hermetically sealed) to prevent pressure leaks inside planes.

Information Checkpoints

Intake Document Checklist

Gathering these files helps us determine the fastest route and approximate cost quotes immediately.

Passport Scan

Deceased's original passport scan and physical photo ID scans of the authorizing next-of-kin.

Coroner Certificate

Official cause of death certificate (must state cause of death is non-infectious/non-infective).

Authorization letter

Signed next-of-kin authorization form authorizing our New Delhi office to handle clearances.

Embassy Clearances

Aviation Packaging Standards

Before boarding caskets on flights, airlines require hermetic seals and custom outer wooden boxes.

WHO Health Seals

Embalming certificates issued by registered doctors in compliance with WHO quarantine instructions.

Hermetic Zinc Liner

Caskets must be sealed airtight (hermetically sealed) to prevent pressure leaks inside planes.

Consular Stamps NOC

Embassy passport cancellations and death NOC stamps managed by daily runner dispatches.

Navigating Crisis With Dignity and Professionalism

At Velmora Global, we specialize in providing human remains repatriation services. Headquartered in New Delhi, our organization was built to bridge the gap between families dealing with grief and the complex administrative structures involved in international and domestic remains transit.

Our coordinators manage clearances at foreign consulates, file digital registries on the Government eCARe portal, coordinate embalming with registered medical practitioners, and handle cargo airport terminal boarding.

Why Velmora Global?

  • No Middlemen: Direct contacts with airline cargo lines and mortuary houses.
  • Expert Handlers: Comprehensive knowledge of APHO port health clearances.
  • Administrative Speed: Streamlining files under tight customs and airline timelines.

Workflow Journey

The Execution Framework

We follow a strict, multi-step timeline to guarantee coordination without cargo shipping delays.

1. Case Registration

Intake of passport records, municipal coroner records, and relative authorizations.

2. Custom Clearance

Embassy runners secure canceled passports and Death NOC clearances.

3. Boarding Handoff

IATA-certified casket loading into pressurized, temperature-controlled cargo compartments.

Aviation Logistics

Global Coffin & Casket Specifications

Airlines enforce rigid safety restrictions on casket parameters. We manage compliance standards locally.

Hermetic Inner Sealing

Deceased remains must be locked inside a hermetic zinc casket liner, certified airtight by embalmers to prevent air pressure leaks during commercial flight cargo.

Outer Coffin Box Crate

caskets must be securely packaged inside a custom fabricated outer wooden box to shield remains and prevent damages inside air cargo holds.

Fumigation Clearance

The wooden transport shell must have a standard fumigation certificate and seal stamp confirming it complies with international phytosanitary customs laws.

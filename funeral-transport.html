Ground Transit Control

Specialized Hearse Fleets & Road Transport

Moving deceased remains by road within India requires specialized vehicles equipped with cold-storage refrigeration or dry-ice cabinets to prevent physical deterioration. Ground transit over long distances is highly regulated, requiring municipal transit certificates, embalming confirmations, and police NOCs when crossing state borders (such as Delhi to Uttar Pradesh or Haryana).

Velmora Global International coordinates a network of specialized, air-conditioned hearse vans and ambulances. We manage all municipal permits, secure border clearances, and provide real-time location logs for the family. Whether transporting remains from an airport terminal directly to your home town or coordinating a local cremation run, our drivers operate with absolute dignity.

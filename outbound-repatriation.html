Process Overview

Logistics, Embalming & Casket Compliance

For foreign nationals deceased in India, or Indian nationals whose final rites are to be held abroad, outbound repatriation requires coordinating clearances across municipal, police, health, and diplomatic desks within India. The remains must be embalmed, placed in a hermetically sealed zinc-lined coffin, and secured in a custom-built outer wooden shipping crate (matching airline safety regulations).

Velmora Global operates directly from New Delhi to coordinate these clearances. We secure Municipal Death Certificates, Police No Objection Certificates (NOC), Embalming certificates from registered medical embalmers, and liaise with the receiving country's embassy to obtain entry approvals and human remains visas.

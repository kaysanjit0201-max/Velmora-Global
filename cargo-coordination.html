IATA Regulations & HUM Booking Codes

Securing cargo hold space for human remains on commercial airlines requires compliance with international air transport protocols. Airlines categorize remains under the cargo handling code HUM (Human Remains), enforcing strict packaging, weight limits, and flight connection constraints. Booking requires a finalized Airway Bill (AWB), APHO clearances, and coordination with flight captains before loading.

Velmora Global coordinates directly with major air carrier freight offices (including Emirates SkyCargo, British Airways World Cargo, and Air India Cargo). We manage container drop-offs at cargo bays, monitor connection flight transits, and track updates in real-time. This ensures remains travel on direct, climate-regulated routes, avoiding delays or temperature compromise.
